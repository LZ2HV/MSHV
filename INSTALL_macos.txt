MSHV for macOS — Installation Notes
=====================================

1. Open the DMG and copy MSHV.app to /Applications.

This application is not signed with an Apple Developer certificate.
macOS (Gatekeeper) will block it on first launch.

2. To remove the quarantine flag, open Terminal and run:

    sudo xattr -cr /Applications/MSHV.app

After that, the app will launch normally without warnings.
